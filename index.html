Who we are

We’re neighbours, colleagues, students, and dreamers united by a single idea: fitness should feel like home. From early jogs around Canary Hill to evening cooldowns by the dams, LOCAL MILES is weaving a culture of movement across Hazaribagh.

Every session is designed to be welcoming—whether you’re lacing up for the first time or chasing a new personal best. With a growing crew of volunteers, we share routes, pace each other, and celebrate every finish line.
